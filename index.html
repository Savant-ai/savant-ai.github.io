ABOUT US

The world is going through hardships every moment of existence - from natural disasters to pandemics to social riots and many more. There are millions getting affected amidst all the haphazard and we aim at making their life better. In this era of technology, finding information on organisations working for the betterment of life are a few clicks. We are here to make the process simpler with more information in lesser clicks.

OUR MISSION

Towards a brighter future, one ₹ at a time

We at BharatNeedsYou aim to work towards a better India by providing the world all the necessary information required for donating to any cause happening in almost all the regions across India. Since this is our start of the journey, we are still working on including the information on various NGOs and other volunteer works from all states and union territories of India. We hope that with this initiative, you can find it simpler to donate even one rupee to the needful.

OUR STORY

The team behind BharatneedsYou are a group of students studying in the Netherlands far away from our homeland India. Covid-19 pandemic has cost the lives of many of our dear ones and known people. We were constantly trying to find sources through which we could be of help to India. Being far away from the country has given us the source of giving monetary help to those in need. Finding a website through which foreign dwellers could donate without being conned was a tedious and time consuming task. Eventually, we came up with the idea that why don’t we come up with a website that is a one stop solution for all information on NGOs - about them, Donation acceptance region, payment methods; volunteering initiatives and also helplines at the time of emergency. At this beginning stage, we are focussing on the most needed Covid-19 helplines, relief funds and NGOs. We hope to include all social cause upfronts in the journey forward and make India a better place for the needy.

WHY THIS INITIATIVE?

Every day the world is facing one kind or the other social trouble. India is one country that has a high poverty rate.There are millions going through hard times with regard to a safe dwelling place, hygienic amenities, clothes, food and getting treatment for illness. Moreover, India’s poverty rate has shot up since the pandemic started. To help these deserving people, there are a number of organisations including NGOs and volunteering initiatives in different parts of India. Almost all of them focus on one social cause and work towards it. People from other countries actively help these organisations monetarily or by providing other resources like clothes, food items etc. Ever since the Foreign Contribution (Regulation) Act(FCRA) of India came into existence, it has been difficult to help the organisations from other countries. Getting information on the trusted organisations that are registered under the FCRA is a long process.
